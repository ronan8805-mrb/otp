OTP AIRWAYS Passenger Data Manifest

Your Data, Your Flight Path

OTP Airways ("Over The Pond," "we," "us") respects your privacy like a first-class lounge respects silence. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our International Terminal, how we use it, and your rights as a passenger.

Information We Collect

  • Boarding credentials: Age verification data (date of birth) stored locally in your browser session — not transmitted to our servers unless you submit a form.
  • Frequent Flyer activity: Quiz responses, OTP Miles, and TRIPS counter simulations stored in your browser's local storage.
  • Flight manifests: Order tracking numbers you enter to check flight status.
  • Automatic logs: Standard analytics data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) if analytics tools are enabled.
  • Communications: Email, name, or contact info if you subscribe to our Frequent Flyer Club or contact support.

How We Use Your Information

  • Verify age compliance (21+) before terminal access.
  • Process orders and provide flight-status (shipping) updates.
  • Improve your in-terminal experience and personalize route recommendations.
  • Send promotional boarding passes, new route announcements, and loyalty rewards (with your consent).
  • Comply with applicable cannabis regulations and law enforcement requests where legally required.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your passenger data. We may share information with:

  • Licensed retail partners and fulfillment carriers to complete your duty-free purchases.
  • Payment processors (Shopify and affiliated gateways) for secure transactions.
  • Analytics and email service providers under strict data-processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

Cookies & Local Storage

Our terminal uses browser session storage for age verification and local storage for gamification features. Third-party cookies may be used for analytics and Shopify checkout. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some terminal features may not function.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including California CPRA), you may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your personal data. Contact us at privacy@otp-airways.com to submit a data request. California residents may also opt out of certain data sharing practices.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your manifest data. No transmission over the internet is 100% secure — fly with trusted connections only.

Children

OTP Airways does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 21. Our terminal is strictly adults-only.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here with a revised effective date. Continued use of the terminal constitutes acceptance.
