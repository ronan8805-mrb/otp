OTP AIRWAYS California Route Advisory

Proposition 65 Warning

California's Proposition 65 requires businesses to provide warnings about significant exposures to chemicals that cause cancer, birth defects, or other reproductive harm. Cannabis products sold by OTP Airways may trigger these requirements.

⚠️ WARNING: This product can expose you to chemicals including cannabis smoke and Δ9-tetrahydrocannabinol (Δ9-THC), which are known to the State of California to cause birth defects or other reproductive harm. For more information go to www.P65Warnings.ca.gov/cannabis

Cannabis Smoke Warning

Some OTP products involve inhalation of cannabis vapor or smoke. Cannabis smoke is on the Proposition 65 list as a chemical known to cause cancer.

THC & Reproductive Harm

Δ9-THC is listed under Proposition 65 as known to cause reproductive harm. Women who are pregnant, planning to become pregnant, or breastfeeding should not use cannabis products. Exposure during pregnancy may affect fetal development.

Additional Chemicals

Depending on the specific product and batch, cannabis and cannabis-derived products may contain trace levels of other substances on the Proposition 65 list, including but not limited to:

  • Carbon monoxide (from combustion, if applicable)
  • Myclobutanil and other agricultural compounds (trace residual from cultivation)
  • Lead and other heavy metals (from environmental exposure during growing)

OTP works with licensed manufacturers who test products for contaminants. Batch-specific lab results are available upon request.

Who This Applies To

This warning is provided to California residents and any passenger departing from or receiving shipments within California. If you are outside California, additional state-specific warnings may apply.

OTP California Compliance

  • All products are sold through licensed California cannabis retailers where required.
  • Products bear the universal cannabis symbol and required government warning statements.
  • Packaging includes net weight, ingredients, batch number, and manufacturer information.
  • CA warning symbol displayed on teal packaging spine and product pages.

More Information
